## Extension to Beyond-PBE Electronic Structure
Current band structure calculations were performed using PBE.
As a next step, hybrid functionals (HSE06) or GW calculations
would be required to obtain quantitatively accurate band gaps,
especially for comparison with experimental results.

## Phonon and Lattice Stability Analysis
For 2D materials such as WSe2, phonon dispersion calculations
would be necessary to assess the dynamical stability
of different structural phases beyond total energy comparison.

## Scaling Atomistic Simulations via MLFF
While ab initio MD is limited in time and length scales,
MLFF enables large-scale simulations with near-DFT accuracy.
Future work includes testing transferability and error indicators
for phase transitions and defect dynamics.
