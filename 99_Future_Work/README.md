# Future Work

This directory outlines potential future directions building upon
the calculations presented in this portfolio.

These items are included to demonstrate awareness of current methodological
limitations and to indicate natural next steps for further research,
rather than to propose independent research projects.

---

## Beyond-DFT Electronic Structure

- Apply hybrid functionals (e.g., HSE06) to improve band gap predictions
  for Si bulk and WSe₂ monolayers.

- Perform GW calculations to obtain more accurate quasiparticle band
  structures for low-dimensional semiconductors.

---

## Lattice Dynamics

- Compute phonon dispersion relations using density functional perturbation
  theory (DFPT) or finite-displacement methods to assess vibrational
  stability and temperature-dependent effects.

- Evaluate dynamical stability of the 1T phase of WSe₂.

---

## Finite-Temperature Effects

- Extend static DFT calculations to ab initio molecular dynamics (AIMD)
  for larger supercells and longer simulation times.

- Investigate thermal effects on phase stability and electronic properties.

---

## Data-Driven Interatomic Potentials

- Generate DFT training datasets for selected materials systems.

- Explore machine-learned force fields to enable large-scale simulations
  while retaining near-DFT accuracy.

---

## Scope

These directions represent methodological extensions of the current work
rather than new research themes.

They are intended as conceptual next steps that would be pursued under
graduate-level supervision.
