## Convergence Strategy
ENCUT and k-point convergence tests were performed on the initial structure
to ensure total energy accuracy within ~2.5 × 10⁻³ eV per cell
(corresponding to ~1 meV/atom).

## Structural Comparison
The 2H and 1T phases differ in local coordination,
leading to qualitatively different electronic structures.

## Electronic Structure Interpretation
The 2H phase exhibits a band gap, whereas the 1T phase shows metallic behavior,
as reflected in both band structure and DOS near the Fermi level.

## Limitations
SOC and many-body effects are not included,
which may affect quantitative band gap values.

## Next Steps
Inclusion of SOC and phonon calculations
to assess quantitative accuracy and dynamical stability.
