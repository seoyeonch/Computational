# Methodological Notes

This directory contains short notes documenting methodological aspects of
my computational work.

These notes focus on:
- Convergence strategies in DFT calculations
- Practical limitations of standard DFT
- Assumptions behind classical force fields and MD
- Theoretical background and limitations of machine-learned force fields

The purpose of these notes is to demonstrate methodological understanding
and critical awareness of the tools used, rather than to provide tutorial-style
introductions.

These notes reflect my emphasis on physical interpretation and numerical
reliability over black-box usage of simulation codes.
