## Context
This PVDF simulation was performed as an additional MD exercise
to explore polymer deformation and dipole behavior.
It is included as supplementary experience and is not a primary research focus.

## What Was Learned
- Construction of multi-stage MD workflows (equilibration → deformation)
- Interpretation of polymer-scale observables such as dipole moment and radius of gyration
- Practical limitations of classical force-field simulations

## Relation to My Main Research
This MD example complements my primary interest in
DFT-based electronic structure calculations,
by providing experience in atomistic simulations at larger length scales.
